	Adaptive only changes at the breakpoints whereas fullt responsive moves with the the page resizing.
2. Describe what it means to be mobile first vs desktop first.
	Mobile first is designing a webpage to be optimized in mobile view where your highest traffic would be.

	Changes from 16px to 10px.
